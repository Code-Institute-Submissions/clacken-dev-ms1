Classes designed with your goals in mind

At Gym Planet we have a variety of classes to suit all fitness levels.

Choose from the available classes below or schedule a one-to-one fitness session with one of our experienced fitness instructors.

Class Types

Spin

Spin classes are the perfect cardio workout to incorporate into any routine. Fun, fast and full of motivational music, these classes are perfect for beginners and experience members alike. Classes are 45 minutes in duration, including warm up and cool down routines. Classes are supervised at all times by our coaching staff who will make sure that every member gets the most possible benefit from the class.

Spin classes will be recommended to some users based on their 10-week workout plan but all members should feel free to chat with our staff about the benefits of including it in whatever workout they are currently undertaking.


Experience Level: Beginner to Advanced


Class day: Mondays and Wednesdays

HIIT Max

HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) is, as the name suggests, an intense workout which you may know as just interval training or circuit training. These explosive workouts take place in short intervals of 20 seconds or more followed by brief rests in between exercises. The benefits are the delivery of explosive energy and improved speed. These workouts are ideal for athletes looking to improve performance in these areas and are more suited to intermediate or advanced members.

HIIT classes last approximately 30 minutes including a warm up and cool down period and are supervised and instructed by our highly trained and certified coaches to ensure that each member gets the maximum benefit from the class at the same time as ensuring that members exercise safely and at the appropriate level.


Experience Level: Intermediate to Advanced


Class day: Tuesdays & Thursdays

Strength & Conditioning

Our Strength & Conditioning classes are ideal for members looking to improve their overall strength and lifting ability. They will experience imporoved performance in their health overall as well as in the areas of power, coordination and speed. Our classes incorporate low, medium and high-intensity exercises to target every muscle group using traditional and functional strength exercises.

Our Strength & Conditioning classes will be recommended on certain members' 10-week workout routines but are equally suited to any of our members looking to build muscle, strength and power. These classes endure for approximately 45 minutes including a warm-up and cool-down routine and are led by our experienced coaches who supervise all members of the class to ensure exercises are perfomed safely with the optimal technique.


Experience Level: Intermediate to Advanced


Class Day: Fridays & Sundays

Combat Fitness

Our combat fitness classes are a fun and energetic way of incorporating both cardio and strength training into your workout. These classes are perfect for anybody looking to burn some extra fat at the same time as build up muscle as they provide a high-energy exercise set which is focused on specific muscle groups. Our fitness instructors coach members through a series of combat-style exercises which include boxing, kick-boxing, wrestling and timed intervals of sparring and cardio.

Our Combat Fitness classes typically last around 45 minutes including a warm-up and cool-down period and are well suited to members of all experience levels. Our fitness instructors ensure that movements and exercises performed are suitable for all levels and teach several versions of the same exercise which members can choose based on their fitness and experience level.


Experience Level: Beginner to Advanced


Class Day: Saturday
