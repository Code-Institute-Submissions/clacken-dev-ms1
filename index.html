What We Offer

Professional Coaching

Our gym offers the training and assistance of super professional and certified personal fitness trainers at all times. You can book a session with any of them at any time or simply walk in and grab one if they are free. One-to-one classes are included free in your membership subject to fair usage and should certainly be used as frequently as is necessary.

Meal/Workout Plans

At Gym World we believe that the only way to progress is to have a plan of action. That's why we offer our members custom-made meal plans and workout plans designed specially for each individual. Workout plans are designed for a 10-week period and are then updated after a progress assessment. Meal-plans are tailor made for each member based on the reccomend caloric intake and feature easy to make and easy to eat meals.

Diverse Fitness Classes

Our gym offers our members a variety of different fitness classes which focus on different areas of development and provide the perfect supplement to their workout plans. These classes are coached by our fitness instructors and each member thus benefits from professional supervision throughout the duration of the class.

Our fitness classes are run multiple times throughout the week, with classes for all experience levels

Meet Our Fully Certified Personal Trainers

Rafael Garcia

Rafael is originally from Spain and has been working with us here at Gym World for the last 3 years. He began his fitness studies in Madrid where he graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Nutrition and Fitness Science. He trains for marathons and tri-athlons in his spare time and can usually be found in the gym even when not working.

Gabriel Gonzales

Gabriel studied Strength and Conditioning in his home country of the Philipines where he graduated top of his class. He went on to pursue a Master's Degree in Exercise Science and has been working with us at our gym for the last 2 years. He leads classes frequently and is very helpful and knowledgeable about all the subjects related to our classes.

Sophia Johnson

Sophia took part in Gymnastics from the age of 6 in her home town of Omaha, Nebraska. She competed at an international level and has won many awards in competitions as well as taking part as a judge in a wide variety of contests and competitions. She can often be found teaching spin classes and is passionate about mixing fun and fitness.

Michael O'Connor

Michael graduated from DCU in Dublin with a Bachelor's Degree in Sports Science and Physical Education. He started his fitness career with teaching at a secondary level before moving on to coach the Leinster Rugby squad. He has been coaching at Gym World for the past 6 months and brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to our team.
